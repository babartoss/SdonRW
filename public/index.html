Indochinese Style Betting

How to Play

Welcome to Indochinese Style Betting. Here's how it works:

  • Choose one or more 2-digit numbers (00-99).
  • Set your bet amount per position (minimum 0.2 USDC, maximum 100 USDC).
  • Your number(s) will be checked against the last two digits of the special prize and the four seventh prizes from the XSMB lottery.
  • If your number matches any of these five positions, you win.
  • The odds are 1:60 per position.
  • Payouts are made at 9:00 AM the next day (2:00 AM UTC).
  • Results are announced daily at 18:15 (11:15 UTC).

Note: A fixed fee of 0.10 USDC is charged per betting session.
